Jackson School of Geosciences Hackathon 2024

Welcome to the documentation of the Jackson School of Geosciences Hackathon. This event focused on promoting open-source software practices to enhance reproducibility and fostering innovation within geoscientific research.

Event Summary

The hackathon took place on October 4th through October 6th and brought together students, faculty, and industry professionals to collaborate on exciting projects.
